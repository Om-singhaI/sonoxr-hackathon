What this is — and what we don't claim.
This is a real 2D echocardiogram (a cine of a beating heart) from the public EchoNet 3d-echo dataset. The released files are 2D + time, not 3D volumes, so we faithfully play the real clip rather than fabricate a 3D mesh. The cardiac motion and rhythm are real, and ED/ES are approximate timing. We deliberately do NOT report an LV trace, fractional-area-change, or ejection fraction — a clinically meaningful chamber border needs a trained segmentation model (the approach CAMUS/EchoNet use), not raw pixel thresholds. Not a medical device.
